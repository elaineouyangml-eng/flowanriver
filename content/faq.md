**What is FlowAnRiver?**
FlowAnRiver is an independent blog about AI, crypto, and the ideas shaping how we live and work. It's written by Elaine — someone who thinks out loud, builds in public, and is figuring things out as she goes.

**Who writes here?**
Everything on FlowAnRiver is written by Elaine. No ghostwriters, no AI-generated articles — just one person's perspective on things she finds genuinely interesting.

**What topics do you cover?**
Mostly AI, cryptocurrency, Web3, and the occasional personal essay. The focus isn't on breaking news — it's on making sense of what's actually happening and what it means for regular people.

**How often do you publish?**
There's no fixed schedule. New posts go up when there's something worth saying.

**Can I republish or translate your content?**
Please reach out first. Original content on this site is not for republication without permission.
